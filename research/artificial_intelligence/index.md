# <i class="fas fa-crosshairs"></i> **Artificial Intelligence Research**


### *A.I. based Robot Control*    
We conduct AI-driven research for human-robot collaboration. Our work focuses on human intent recognition, robot intelligence, LLM- and VLM-based robot control, and deep learning- and reinforcement learning-based robot control. Through these efforts, we aim to enable robots to understand human needs and respond in real time, realizing more natural and effective human-robot collaboration.
