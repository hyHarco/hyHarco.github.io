On December 30, 2025, **HARCO Lab** held a farewell party to wrap up the year and say goodbye to members starting new chapters. 

The event was a meaningful time for all members to gather and reflect on the achievements of 2025. We expressed our deepest gratitude to the departing members for their dedication and significant contributions to the lab's research and collaborative projects. 

The evening was filled with warm conversations, sharing memories from the past year, and wishing each other success in future academic and professional endeavors.
